Our mission

Education should be accessible to everyone

We believe that where you come from should never limit where you can go. That's why every service we offer — from your first counselling call to the day you arrive on campus — is completely free for students.

We're funded by our university partners, not by you. This means our recommendations are based on what's right for you, not on commission.
