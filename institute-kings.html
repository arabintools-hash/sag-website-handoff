Courses at Kings Education

Detailed breakdown of every English programme available — including hours, levels and content.

Most popular

General English

20 lessons/week (15 hrs)All levels A1–C2Start any Mondayfrom £300/week

The most flexible course at Kings. Three hours of lessons each day covering speaking, listening, reading and writing through real-world topics. Class sizes of maximum 14 students. Ideal for learners who want steady progress with free time to explore their new city. Available at all UK and US campuses year-round.

Intensive

Intensive English

28 lessons/week (21 hrs)20 core + 8 electivesAll levelsfrom £370/week

A fast-track to fluency combining 20 core English lessons with 8 specialised elective modules each week. Electives include Conversation & Pronunciation, English for Work, Current Affairs, Creative Writing, British Culture, and more. Maximum 14 per class. Includes the Kings Learning Progress Guarantee for courses of 12+ weeks.

Long-term

Diploma of Intensive English

28 lessons/week24–48 weeksAll levelsfrom £290/week

Immerse yourself in English for six months to a full year. This long-term intensive programme delivers deep fluency through sustained study, with the same elective structure as Intensive English. Ideal for students who want to live, learn and think like a native English speaker. Includes regular progress reviews and a Kings Diploma on completion.

Exam prep

IELTS Preparation

28 lessons/week (21 hrs)Min. B1 levelUK schoolsfrom £370/week

Combines 20 lessons of core English with 8 lessons of dedicated IELTS exam strategies. Covers all four papers: Listening, Reading, Writing and Speaking. Regular mock exams, individual feedback and band-score tracking. Kings Oxford is also an official IELTS Test Centre — you can prepare and sit the exam in the same building.

Exam prep

Super Intensive IELTS

2–3 weeksMin. B1 levelOxford£495/week

A short, high-intensity boot camp designed for students who need to raise their IELTS score quickly. 20 core lessons plus 12 IELTS-focused sessions per week. Concentrated practice on exam techniques, timing strategies and task response across all four skills. Ideal just before a test date.

Exam prep

TOEFL Preparation

28 lessons/weekMin. B1 levelBoston & New Yorkfrom $535/week

Dedicated training for the TOEFL iBT exam, accepted by US and Canadian universities. Covers all four sections: Reading, Listening, Speaking and Writing. Learn test-taking strategies, practise with authentic materials and track your projected score. Available at Kings US campuses (Boston and New York); an Intensive TOEFL option runs in Boston.

Exam prep

Cambridge English (FCE / CAE)

28 lessons/week12-week course · OxfordMin. B2 / C1£4,680 full course

Rigorous preparation for Cambridge First (B2) or Advanced (C1) certificates — recognised by 25,000+ employers and universities worldwide. Covers all five papers with intensive practice, timed mock exams and expert teacher feedback. Fixed start dates aligned with official exam sessions.

Professional

Business English

28 lessons/weekMin. B2 levelLondonfrom £475/week

English Plus Business, Marketing & Finance: 20 core lessons plus 8 specialist business modules. Covers meetings, presentations, negotiations, report writing, financial vocabulary and marketing communications. Small classes with case-study methodology. Ideal for professionals and pre-MBA students.

Creative

English Plus Art & Design

28 lessons/weekMin. B1 levelLondon · Oxford · Brightonfrom £475/week

Combine English tuition with art and design workshops. Build a portfolio while improving your language skills — covering drawing, painting, photography, graphic design and mixed media. Taught at Kings London and Brighton campuses with access to studio facilities.

Creative

English Plus Film

28 lessons/weekMin. B1 levelLondon & New Yorkfrom £475/week

Learn English through the lens of filmmaking. Workshops cover scriptwriting, camera work, editing and post-production alongside core English lessons. Produce your own short film by the end of the course. Available at Kings London and New York.

Specialist

English for Law

28 lessons/weekMin. B2 levelLondon & Oxfordfrom £475/week

Designed for law students and professionals. Covers legal English terminology, case analysis, mooting, contract language and courtroom communication alongside core English fluency lessons. Available at Kings London and Oxford.

Pathway

University Preparation (UK)

Full-time · 1–3 termsMin. B1 level£8,400/term

A structured academic pathway combining English language development with study skills, IELTS preparation and subject-specific modules. On successful completion, students receive guaranteed conditional offers from Kings partner universities across the UK. Includes personal statement support and UCAS guidance.

Pathway

University Preparation (USA)

Full-time · GO: PreparedMin. B1 level$7,524/term

Prepares international students for degree study at US universities. Covers academic English, TOEFL preparation, American academic culture, essay writing and research skills. Pathway agreements with partner universities for conditional admission on completion.
