Why the UK

Six reasons students choose the UK

World-class prestige

Oxford, Cambridge and 150+ universities consistently ranked among the best on earth.

Shorter, cheaper degrees

Three-year bachelor's and one-year master's mean lower total cost and faster entry to work.

Work while you study

Up to 20 hours a week during term, full-time in holidays — helping cover living costs.

Scholarships & loans

A wide range of merit and need-based funding — and we help you find every one you qualify for.

Gateway to Europe

Low-cost flights and rail put most of Europe within a few hours for weekend travel.

Multicultural & welcoming

A diverse society where students from across the Arab world feel at home from day one.
