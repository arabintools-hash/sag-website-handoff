When to apply — intakes & deadlines by country

Most universities open more than one intake a year. Here are the main entry points for each destination, plus a recommended timeline so you never miss a deadline.

Plan backwards

Your recommended timeline

A rough guide counting back from a September start. We adjust it to your destination and deadlines.

10–12 months before

Explore & shortlist

Decide your destination and field, shortlist universities, and check entry requirements. Try the country quiz and comparison table.

8–10 months before

Sit your English test

Book and take IELTS or TOEFL, and start drafting your statement of purpose. Results are valid for two years.

6–8 months before

Apply to universities

Submit applications with your documents and references. We prepare and send everything, and chase offers.

4–6 months before

Accept your offer & scholarships

Choose your university, apply for scholarships, and pay any deposit to secure your place.

2–4 months before

Finances & visa

Set up your proof of funds (Sperrkonto, GIC, blocked account), gather documents, and submit your visa application.

2–4 weeks before

Accommodation & travel

Confirm housing, book flights, arrange insurance and airport pickup, and attend your pre-departure briefing.
