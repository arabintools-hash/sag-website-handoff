Why Australia

Six reasons students choose Australia

World-class universities

The Group of Eight and many more rank among the global best, with strong research and teaching.

Affordable, with scholarships

Lower total cost than the US, plus a wide range of scholarships and part-time work for students.

Work while you study

Up to 48 hours per fortnight during term, and unlimited hours during scheduled breaks.

2–4 years post-study work

The Temporary Graduate visa (subclass 485) lets you stay and work after you finish.

Pathway to residency

Skilled migration programs offer graduates a recognised route to permanent residency.

High quality of life

Sydney and Melbourne rank among the world's most liveable cities — safe, sunny and welcoming.
